My to 3 movies of all time

Prison Break

Prison Break is an American crime drama television series created by Paul Scheuring for Fox. The series revolves around two brothers: Lincoln Burrows (Dominic Purcell) and Michael Scofield (Wentworth Miller); Lincoln has been sentenced to death for a crime he did not commit, while Michael devises an elaborate plan to help his brother escape prison and clear his name

Game of Thrones

Game of Thrones is an American fantasy drama television series created by David Benioff and D.B. Weiss for HBO. The series is based on the A Song of Ice and Fire novel series by George R.R. Martin, and follows several royal families as they compete for control of the Iron Throne in the fictional continents of Westeros and Essos.

Breaking Bad

Breaking Bad is an American crime drama television series created and produced by Vince Gilligan. The series follows Walter White (Bryan Cranston), a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer, and his former student and business partner, Jesse Pinkman (Aaron Paul). The show explores themes of morality, family, and the consequences of one's actions as Walter descends into the criminal underworld.
